cDESP

A compliance platform for industrial refrigeration pressure equipment, built from scratch for DCRR.

← All projects

cDESP digitalises the end-to-end compliance workflow for refrigeration Pressure Equipment (Équipements Sous Pression, or ESP). Built by Wabel Group for industrial refrigeration specialist DCRR, the platform replaces spreadsheet chaos with a single source of truth for DESP regulatory obligations.

The tool tracks every pressure vessel in a client’s fleet, schedules inspections ahead of regulatory deadlines, stores all supporting paperwork (CTP 2020 forms, PED certificates, maintenance reports), and coordinates the stakeholders, from operators to inspectors and regulators, across a single multi-tenant SaaS interface.

As CEO of Wabel Group, I led product, engineering and delivery on this one: from scoping with DCRR’s compliance team, to shipping a stack that holds up in the field.

What cDESP does

  1. Equipment registry. Every ESP in a client’s fleet tracked with full specifications, location, service history and regulatory status.
  2. Scheduled inspections. Automatic alerts before CTP / DESP deadlines, so compliance work is planned, not reactive.
  3. Document vault. CTP 2020 forms, PED certificates, inspection reports and maintenance logs centralised and searchable.
  4. Multi-tenant access. DCRR’s clients log in and self-serve. Technicians update data in the field, compliance leads audit from the office.
  5. Regulation-aware. Built around the French Cahier Technique Professionnel (CTP 2020) and the EU Pressure Equipment Directive (PED/DESP).
